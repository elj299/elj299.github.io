I am a graduate student in the Ecology, Evolution and Behavior Department at the University of Texas at Austin. Prior to coming to Austin, I graduated from the University of Arizona with a degree in Mathematics with minors in Biology and Art History.

I study the evolution of viruses and the biophysical mechanisms of protein evolution using computational methods.
