Eleisha Jackson

I am a 3rd year graduate student in the Ecology, Evolution and Behavior Department at the University of Texas at Austin. Prior to coming to Austin, I graduated from the University of Arizona with a degree in Mathematics with minors in Biology and Art History.

I study the evolution of viruses and the biophysical mechanisms of protein evolution using computational methods.

When I am not doing science, I enjoy art and volunteering at a local museum. I also love watching sports and sometimes blog about it at Dimes and Delight.
